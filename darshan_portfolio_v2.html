Key Research

Dual-Stimuli Nano Elicitation in Annona muricata
Green Nanoparticle Synthesis · Phytochemistry · Drug Discovery Applications
  • Designed a multi-treatment study applying Zn–Ag bimetallic nanoparticles, CuO nanoparticles, and biochar to elicit secondary metabolites in a high-value medicinal plant
